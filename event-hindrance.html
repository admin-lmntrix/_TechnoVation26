Hindrance

Rapid Coding Event

A rapid coding contest run over three consecutive rounds — only two of your three teammates sit at the machines in any given round.

TrackOff-Stage
Team3 per team
Classes9–12
LanguagesPython · Java · C++
Rounds3

The Brief

A rapid coding event for teams of three, run over three consecutive rounds. Two members compete in each round, and you choose how to rotate the third in.

Format

  • Round 1 — story-based problem solving.
  • Round 2 — a surprise “switch” round, revealed only on the day.
  • Round 3 — a rapid coding sprint.
  • Only two of the three members sit for any round; you may rotate the third in and switch mid-round, but members may not confer between themselves.

Kit & Rules

  • Permitted languages: Python, Java or C++.

All participants report at 7:30 AM in proper school uniform, with a valid School ID and a Teacher-in-Charge. Read the full guidelines →
