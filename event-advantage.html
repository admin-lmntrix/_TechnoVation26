Advantage

Digital Marketing Event

Build a complete digital-marketing campaign for an assigned product, survive an on-the-spot crisis twist, then pitch to the judges.

TrackOn-Stage
Team2 per team
Classes11–12
Entry1 team / school
Deck≤ 8 slides

The Brief

Design a complete digital-marketing campaign for a product or service assigned beforehand. Each school may send one team; products and problem statements are pre-assigned to ensure depth and creativity.

The Campaign

  • Brand name & tagline.
  • Unique Selling Proposition (USP).
  • Target audience identification.
  • Social-media campaign (Instagram, etc.).
  • A basic marketing plan.
  • An influencer / collaboration plan.
  • A PowerPoint of no more than 8 slides — submitted 2 days before the event.
  • An on-the-spot crisis challenge, set 1–2 hours before.

Final Presentation

  • No more than 5 minutes (4 + 1), followed by a 2–3 minute Q&A from the judges.
  • Presentations must be clear, creative and time-bound.

Judging

  • Creativity & originality.
  • Understanding of the target audience.
  • Practicality & real-world application.
  • Strategy & execution.
  • Presentation & communication skills.

Rules & Kit

  • Bring your own devices (laptops / phones).
  • Internet usage is for research only.
  • Lack of originality leads to disqualification.
  • Adhere strictly to the time limits; the judges' decisions are final.

All participants report at 7:30 AM in proper school uniform, with a valid School ID and a Teacher-in-Charge. Read the full guidelines →
