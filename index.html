The Institution

A House of Learning

The Theme · MMXXVI

Greeks to Geeks

It started in the courtyards of Athens.

Greek philosophers built logic and geometry with reason alone. Twenty-five centuries on, that same thinking runs inside a processor. Greeks to Geeks follows the thread from myth to machine.

Our emblem says it: a stag built from circuitry, antlers branching like signal traces.

What Awaits

Four Pillars of the Festival

I

Off-Stage Events

Thirteen off-stage arenas — rapid coding, hackathon, AI, robotics, gaming, data, web and film — running across the labs and studios all day.

II

On-Stage Events

Three events under the lights — the IT Quiz, the application pitch, and the digital-marketing showcase — contested before a live audience.

III

The Rep's Event

Grid-Lock — a multi-stage digital trail that every participating school's two representatives must face. Mandatory, and unmissable.

IV

One Electric Day

A single day, one campus, fifteen events. From the 7:30 reporting bell to the final results — Greeks to Geeks, start to finish.

Will you join the evolution?

Registration is open to schools across the city. Assemble your team, choose your events, and take your place in the agora.
