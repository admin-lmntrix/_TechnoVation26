IT Quiz · Junior & Senior

The IT Quiz, run in Junior and Senior divisions — prelims decide the six finalists who play on stage.

TrackOn-Stage
Team3 per team
DivisionsJr 6–8 · Sr 9–12
StagePrelims → Finals

The Brief

An IT quiz covering aspects of the IT world, run in two divisions — Junior (classes 6–8) and Senior (9–12). Each school sends a team of 3 students, from any classes within the division's limit, for both events.

Format

  • Preliminary rounds for both the Junior and Senior quizzes.
  • The top 6 teams from each prelim advance to the On-Stage Finals.

Rules

  • Any use of unfair or prohibited means leads to disqualification.
  • The Quizmaster's decision is final and binding.

All participants report at 7:30 AM in proper school uniform, with a valid School ID and a Teacher-in-Charge. Read the full guidelines →
