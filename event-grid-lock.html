The Brief

Grid-Lock is the representatives' event. Teams solve a series of digital clues and challenges, with the event proceeding in multiple stages — each one unlocking the next.

Participation is a mandate for every participating school; both of a school's representatives take part.

What it tests

  • Logical reasoning.
  • Technical skill.
  • Speed.
  • Devices are mandatory.
